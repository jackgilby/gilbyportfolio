iD Software Rebrand

This rebranding project was created for a Graphic Design II course that asked students to pick a company for a comprehensive redesign. iD Software is a video game company well known for the DOOM franchise of games. The rebranding focused on creating a brand image that conveys the gritty style of their games while retaining a contemporary look. Aspects of the project were done using Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop and XD. The linked PDF includes the proposal, ideation, sketches, the branding guide, and the mockups.

Faygo Redesign

The Faygo redesign was created for a design class that tasked students with creating a new brand identity and packaging design. The featured mockups include a new logo treatment along with an illustration heavy design that conveys the playful and bold nature of the Faygo brand. The mockups were created using Adobe XD, and the designs were created using Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. The linked PDF includes the process book that features ideation sketches, design drafts and alternate mockups.
